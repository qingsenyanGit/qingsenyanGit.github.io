Biography

Qingsen Yan is currently a Professor in School of Computer Science at Northwestern Polytechnical University, Xi’an, China. He was a Postdoctoral Researcher at the University of Adelaide, working with Prof. Qinfeng (Javen) Shi. He received a PhD in Computer Science from Northwestern Polytechnical University (Xi’an, China) under the supervision of Prof. Yanning Zhang. During Jan. 2018 to Jan. 2019, he was a visiting scholar at Australian Centre for Visual Technologies, University of Adelaide, under the supervision of Prof. Ian Reid and Prof. Qinfeng (Javen) Shi.
